<h1 class='PrivacyPolicyTitle' >Parental Control</h1>

<p class='Details'>
&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp
Parental control on the app will filter out all movies that are not suitable for young viewers. This feature will only allow access to the following categories: Animations, Cartoons, Family, and Religious.
<br>
However, additional suitable categories for young viewers may be added in the future.
<br><br>
To activate parental control on the app, please enter a 5-digit PIN below that you, as a parent, can remember. Then, tap the 'ON' button to activate parental control immediately.
<br>
To remove parental controls, you will need to enter the same 5-digit PIN you used to activate parental control. Type the PIN below and then tap the 'OFF' button to deactivate parental control immediately.

</p>
